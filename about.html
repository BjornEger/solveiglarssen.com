About Solveig

Solveig Larssen is the pen name behind the Triumvirate series.

The author

Solveig Larssen is the pen name of Sune Larsen, a transportation policy advisor, and co‑founder of Almenr, a co‑living business. Sune lives with his spouse, Regitze, in Copenhagen.

The series

Don’t Feed the Beast is the first book in the “Triumvirate” series. It follows Eskild, the Brotherhood Bear. The second and third books will be told from the viewpoints of Eskild’s younger, liberal siblings, Dag and Selma.

Solveig always welcomes input — join the book club and participate in the discussion about your favourite Triumvirate characters and the future of the series.
